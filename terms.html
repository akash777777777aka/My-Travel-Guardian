1. Introduction

Welcome to My Travel Guardian. By commissioning our travel guardianship services, you agree to these distilled protocols that ensure safety, luxury, and cultural stewardship across the Indian subcontinent.
02

Bespoke Curation

Every itinerary is a unique masterpiece. Our guardians commit to providing artisanal travel design. Once an itinerary proposal is accepted, a stewardship fee of 25% is required to initiate logistical securing of heritage properties and private transport.

03

The Guardian's Promise

We pledge to act as your advocates and protectors. However, travel involves variables beyond mortal control—weather, heritage site closures, or logistical shifts. We guarantee proactive problem-solving as your dedicated guardians.

04

Cancellation Protocol

Heritage properties often require significant lead times. Cancellations made 90 days prior to travel receive a full refund minus the stewardship fee. Within 60 days, 50% refund. Within 30 days, fees are non-refundable due to property commitments.

Questions of Protocol?
