Our Story

The Guardian's Heritage

Rooted in Sambalpur,
Open to the World.

Our journey began not in a corporate high-rise, but amidst the rhythmic clatter of handlooms in Sambalpur, Odisha. Here, "Atithi Devo Bhava" — The Guest is God — isn't just a phrase. It's a sacred duty.

I founded My Travel Guardian to bring that same profound sense of care and protection to global luxury travel. Whether you're navigating the souks of Marrakech or the fjords of Norway, we watch over your journey with the warmth of an old friend and the precision of a guardian.

Priya Mohanty

Founder & Chief Guardian
