The Guardian's Heritage

Rooted in Sambalpur, open to the world.

Where "Atithi Devo Bhava" is a Sacred Duty

Our journey began not in a corporate high-rise, but amidst the rhythmic clatter of handlooms in Sambalpur, Odisha. We carry forward the timeless philosophy that the guest is god — and every journey deserves the devotion of a guardian.

My Travel Guardian was born from this cultural richness. Every bespoke itinerary we craft is infused with the warmth, precision, and care inherited from centuries of Odishan hospitality.
